Description
-----------

Open your Task Coach task files on your android device wherever and whenever you need to.

Current features:
• Works with files from Task Coach ver 1.3.36
• Integration with Google Drive opening your task files

Planned Features
• Edit your tasks on your device
• Add new tasks from your phone or tablet
• Multiple Task Coach files can be opened at once
• View your tasks on google calendar
• Manage your tasks even while being offline
• Plan your days ahead with visual scheduling
• Home screen widgets
• Notifications on your Sony Smartwatch
• Google Now and DashClock integration
• Order your tasks by due date/name/create time/priority


Contains In App ads which can be removed with an In App Purchase
